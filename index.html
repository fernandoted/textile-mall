pcfc (ports, custom and
free zone corporation)

PCFC (Ports, Customs and Free Zone Corporation), comprises of Dubai Customs, Dubai Ports Authority, Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority and Trakhees. As a combination of its companies, PCFC is responsible for one of the top ports in the world, offers unique business opportunities and delivers services of the highest quality and value, acting as a facilitator of trade. PCFC Hotels (PCFCH), a wholly-owned subsidiary of PCFC, is the developer of the Textile Mall.

texmas

Texmas, Textile Merchants Group, is the apex body of Dubai wholesale textile merchants. Established in year 1990, the formation of Texams was a landmark development for the textile trade in the UAE. It consists of 460 member organization and collectively represent a trade network across more than 45 countries. For more information, visit: www.texmas.com
